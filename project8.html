Create a motion graphic for the Humber E-Sports team. Cooperate with other creative team members to create motion graphic overlay packages for their live streaming or matches.

Crafting dynamic motion graphics with graphic assets that our team made together. Initially designed in Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop underwent a meticulous process before being imported into Adobe After Effects. Here, I orchestrated the convergence of our graphic assets with motion graphic design, infusing life and movement into the elements to enhance their visual impact and engagement. As the stages reached their culmination, the transition to Adobe After Effects marked a pivotal phase.

My focus was on harmonizing and unifying the pace, ensuring a seamless interplay between meticulously curated visual elements. This involved a deliberate effort to synchronize rhythm and timing. aiming to craft a cohesive and immersive experience for the audience. These overlay motion graphics were created for team's live stream and match.
