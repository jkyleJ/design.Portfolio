Furniture E-commerce Application

Role

  • UI/UX Designer

Project Type

  • Promotional

Tools Used

  • Figma
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop

Team

  • Jeongwon Jin - UI/UX Designer

Duration

This project focuses on creating a native iOS app that adheres to Apple specifications. It plans to create an e-commerce application for displaying and selling furniture. Customers may explore This Project's furniture assortment and make purchases with ease thanks to the app's easy user interface.

The primary objective of this project is to meticulously craft an application interface that places the user at the forefront of the design process. We aim to ensure that every aspect of the interface is tailored to meet the needs and expectations of our target audience. By prioritizing user-centric design principles and aligning them with the overarching business objectives, we strive to create an engaging and purposeful digital experience for our customers.
